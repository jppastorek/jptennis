About Me


I am a member of Lakeside Country Club and have been teaching lessons during the past several months. I taught previously at multiple clubs, with the longest stretch of 4 years at Aurora Country Club. I have been a member of the USPTA for 10 years now, and I am certified as a Level 2 Professional. I currently teach and coach at Christian Brothers School in City Park, and I have several years of experience running different sports camps such as lacrosse and tennis. When I'm not out on the courts or in the classroom, I like to spend time with my two children, Grace (4) and Chloe (2), and my lovely wife, Ami."
